1. Introduction

Welcome to Korean Verbs AI ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our extension.

üîí Privacy First: Your privacy is our top priority. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Local Storage Data

Korean Verbs AI stores the following information locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API:

  • Learning History: Verbs you have studied, including timestamps
  • Theme Preference: Your dark/light mode selection
  • Chat History: Conversations with the AI assistant (stored locally only)

2.2 Information NOT Collected

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Analytics or usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the extension functionality
  • Save your learning progress and preferences
  • Enable the chat and AI features
  • Store your theme preference

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Pollination AI

Korean Verbs AI uses Pollination AI for AI-powered explanations and chat functionality. When you request an AI explanation or chat message:

  • Your request is sent to Pollination AI's servers
  • Pollination AI processes your request and returns a response
  • We do not store your requests on our servers
  • Please review Pollination AI's privacy policy for their data handling practices

4.2 Web Speech API

Pronunciation features use the browser's built-in Web Speech API. This is processed locally on your device and we do not collect any audio data.

5. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device, not on remote servers
  • HTTPS Only: All communication with Pollination AI uses encrypted HTTPS
  • No Tracking: We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • No Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored locally by the extension
  • Delete: Clear your learning history at any time using the "Clear History" button
  • Control: Manage extension permissions through Chrome settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension at any time

7. Data Retention

Learning History: Stored locally until you clear it manually. The extension automatically limits history to the 50 most recent items to manage storage efficiently.

Chat History: Stored locally during your session. Cleared when you close the extension.

Theme Preference: Stored indefinitely until changed or the extension is uninstalled.

8. Children's Privacy

Korean Verbs AI is designed for language learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the extension following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.
